I'm currently a Postdoctoral Scholar - Research Associate at the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles. I'm part of Dr Ewa Deelman's group, I'm working on scientific workflows management on large-scale cyber-infrastrucres. I defended my PhD in Computer Science at ENS Lyon, France, in 2018, under the supervision of Anne Benoit and Yves Robert. I was part of the team ROMA at the LIP.
I was mainly working on co-scheduling algorithms for large-scale applications. I was also working on scheduling and data management problems on the new many-core architectures.
My main interests are:

  • Parallel algorithms
  • Resilience & reliability at Exa-scale era
  • Scheduling problems in High Performance Computing
  • Many-core architectures
  • Scientific Workflows
You can find more information on my CV and on my Google Scholar profile.
